Hello, everyone, and welcome to the Underland! This is a place for everyone to discuss the brilliantly written Underland Chronicles by Suzanne Collins. Here are the rules: 1. Keep it clean - No NSFW content of any kind. Failure to follow this rule will result in a punishment ranging from a warning to an instant ban, depending on the severity. No excuses. You may curse, but you may not use racist or discriminatory terms. 2. Be nice! - Treat others how you'd like to be treated! Try to help others and if you don't have anything nice to say, don't say anything. 3. No discussions about sensitive topics! - For example religion, politics, anything that could cause an argument. And if that does happen... 4. If you find yourself in an argument, try to stop it! - We'd like to keep this server as a fun, happy place where we can have a nice community. 5. No talking about suicide! - That includes joking! If you truly feel this way, please talk to an adult and visit this website; https://suicidepreventionlifeline.org/ 6. No discrimination! - Please try not to hold a grudge or not treat someone equally because of gender, race, or sexual orientation. Please judge people by their actions and nothing else. This should be an accepting community. 7. Don't argue with the moderators - If an unfair punishment is dealt, PM or ping me to let me know and if I feel it's an issue then I will handle it. 8. No copypasta-type messages are allowed in the server, as they create unnecessary spam. This includes awareness-raising ones, like the ones that went around regarding the Blue Whale Challenge or Pridefall. These are not allowed as they frequently spread false information. If you’d like to send a link to a charity or something that’s raising awareness for a topic, contact the moderators for approval so we can see if it’s verifiable. 9. Have fun! - This one's pretty self-explanatory. The punishment depends on the severity of the issue, which is at my or a moderator/administrator’s discretion. (edited)
